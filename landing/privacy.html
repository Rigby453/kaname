Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Kaname collects from you, why we collect it, how we use and store it, and what choices you have. We aim to be plain and honest — no legal fog.

By using Kaname (the app and its web version), you agree to the practices described here. If you have questions, reach us at support.kaname@gmail.com.

1. Who we are

Kaname ("we", "us") is a student productivity planner. The app lets you organise your day, track habits and mood, and — on the paid plan — receive AI-powered schedule suggestions. We operate this service independently; there is no parent corporation or advertising network behind us.

2. What data we collect and why

Account data

To create an account you provide either an email address or a Russian phone number (+7) plus a password. We store this to identify you, let you log in, and recover your account. We do not require a real name.

App content you create

All the content you enter in the app is yours. This includes:

  • Tasks, priorities, and deadlines (Today view and Week plan)
  • Daily diary entries and mood logs
  • Nutrition logs — meals, calories, macros (Phase 1 feature)
  • Sleep and water tracking records
  • Workout and focus-session logs
  • Goals (month / year / multi-year)

We store this data on our servers so you can sync across devices and access it offline. We do not read your diary entries or tasks for advertising or profiling.

Device and usage data

When you use the app we automatically collect minimal technical data: app version, operating system type, anonymised crash reports, and aggregate feature-use counts (e.g., "morning review opened today"). We do not collect precise location, contacts, or device identifiers linked to you personally.

Payment data

If you subscribe to Kaname Premium ($10/month), payment is processed by Apple App Store, Google Play, or another payment provider depending on your platform. We never see or store your full card number. We receive only a subscription status token and receipt confirming you have an active paid plan.

3. How we use your data

  • To provide and sync the app across your devices
  • To power AI features on the Premium plan (see section 4)
  • To send transactional messages — password reset, payment receipts (no marketing unless you opt in)
  • To fix bugs and improve the product using anonymised, aggregated analytics
  • To comply with applicable law

We do not sell your data, and Kaname shows no ads on any tier — free or Premium. We never share your identity with ad networks.

4. AI features and third-party AI providers

AI-powered features (smart plan redistribution, diary insights, pattern analysis) are available on the Premium plan only. To generate these suggestions, we send a structured summary of your relevant data — tasks, timing patterns, diary flags — to an AI provider. Currently we use Google Gemini or Anthropic Claude (whichever is configured at the time; this can change with a server-side update).

This means your task summaries and diary patterns may be processed by Google or Anthropic under their respective data-processing agreements. We send the minimum needed to answer your request; we do not send raw diary text unless you explicitly use a feature that requires it (e.g., "analyse my week"). Both providers are contractually prohibited from training their models on data sent via the API.

AI is never used on the free tier; it is funded only by the Premium subscription, never by ads.

5. Data storage and security

Your data is stored on servers hosted in the European Union (Neon PostgreSQL, EU region). Data in transit is encrypted with TLS 1.2+. Passwords are hashed with bcrypt; we never store them in plain text. We apply the principle of least privilege: only the parts of the system that need your data can access it.

No system is perfectly secure. If we ever discover a breach that affects your personal data, we will notify you promptly and take corrective action.

6. Data sharing and third parties

We share your data only with:

  • AI providers (Gemini / Claude) — task and diary summaries, Premium only, as described in section 4.
  • Payment processors (Apple, Google, or others) — only the data required to complete your transaction.
  • Infrastructure providers — hosting, database, and error-monitoring services that process data on our behalf under data-processing agreements.
  • Law enforcement or courts — only if required by applicable law and only to the extent legally required.

We do not share data with advertisers, data brokers, or analytics resellers.

7. Your rights

You can, at any time:

  • Access — request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct — update incorrect account information in app Settings.
  • Delete — request full deletion of your account and all associated data. See section 8 below.
  • Export — request your task, diary, and nutrition data in a machine-readable format (JSON/CSV). Email us and we will prepare it within 14 days.
  • Object to processing — if we are processing your data on the basis of legitimate interest, you can object and we will stop unless we have a compelling overriding reason.

To exercise any of these rights, email support.kaname@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Account and data deletion

You can delete your account from within the app: go to Settings → Account → Delete account. This permanently removes your account credentials and all content you have created (tasks, diary entries, logs). Deletion is irreversible.

After deletion, anonymised aggregate statistics (e.g., "user was active for N months") may be retained in aggregate form only, with no link back to your identity. Backup copies are purged within 30 days.

9. Children

Kaname is designed for students aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy (e.g., start collecting new types of data or change how we use existing data), we will notify you via in-app notification or email at least 14 days before the change takes effect. Minor editorial updates take effect immediately and are noted with a new "Last updated" date above.
